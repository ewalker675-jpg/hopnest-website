Skylark & Nuthatch

Two shepherd huts above the Teme Valley. Opening 24th July 2026.

Private huts above the valley

Skylark and Nuthatch sit above the Teme Valley with uninterrupted views across rolling Worcestershire countryside. Designed for couples, each shepherd hut combines the simplicity of outdoor living with modern comforts, including a king-size bed, private bathroom, full kitchen and cozy underfloor electric heating. The huts are positioned to offer complete visual privacy from one another, with no shared access and no overlapping sightlines. Outside, enjoy your own private sanctuary—featuring a wood-fired hot tub (Skylark) or outdoor roll-top copper bath (Nuthatch), a suspended stargazing net to watch the dark skies, and a crackling outdoor fire pit (no indoor wood burners, preserving clean air inside your hut).

Note: For friends looking for larger private retreats for groups, both shepherd huts can be booked together to accommodate groups of up to 4 guests, or combined with our Bell Tent Retreat for larger group events.
