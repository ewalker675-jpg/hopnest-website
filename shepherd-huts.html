Skylark & Nuthatch

Two shepherd huts above the Teme Valley. Opening summer 2026.

Private huts above the valley

Skylark and Nuthatch sit above the Teme Valley with uninterrupted views across rolling Worcestershire countryside. Designed for couples, each shepherd hut combines the simplicity of outdoor living with modern comforts, including a king-size bed, private bathroom, kitchen and outdoor fire pit. During the day, enjoy walking, cycling and exploring the local area; in the evening, relax on the stargazing net and take in the dark skies above the valley.

Note: For friends looking for larger private retreats for groups, both shepherd huts can be booked together to accommodate groups of up to 4 guests, or combined with our Bell Tent Retreat for larger group events.
