Discover things to do, walks, craft breweries, and food hotspots near Martley, Worcestershire. Our favorite local recommendations.

Explore Nearby Destinations in Martley & Beyond

Hopnest Retreats sits in a beautiful, quiet corner of the Teme Valley, providing easy access to walks and fantastic local businesses. Here are the highlights you should not miss during your group stay:

Pubs & Dining — 5 Minute Walk

The Talbot in Martley

Located just a short five-minute walk from the campsite, The Talbot is a traditional country pub with a wonderful, spacious beer garden. It is the perfect spot for your group to walk to for a hearty pub lunch, evening meals, or a relaxed pint after a day in nature. Elizabeth's guests highly praise their food and warm service.

Local Craft Brewery

The Hop Shed Brewery

An award-winning craft brewery located very close by in the valley. Built on a certified hop farm, The Hop Shed produces delicious local craft beers directly from Worcester hops. They host regular taproom openings, live music, and street food events, making it a highly popular weekend activity for glamping groups.

Fine Dining & Events

The Green Cow Kitchen

For groups looking to celebrate a milestone, birthday, or hen party, the nearby Green Cow Kitchen offers incredible farm-to-table fine dining events. Located in a beautifully restored barn, they serve creative, locally-sourced menus showcasing the finest ingredients from the Worcestershire countryside. Booking in advance is highly recommended.

Nature & Walks

Teme Valley & Malvern Hills

The Teme Valley is a haven for walkers, with paths winding along the River Teme and through historic cider orchards. For groups wishing to hike further, the spectacular ridge of the Malvern Hills is just a 30-minute drive away, offering panoramic views across Worcestershire and Herefordshire.
