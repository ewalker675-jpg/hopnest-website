Hen Party Glamping in the Worcestershire Countryside

A fully private riverside meadow, five furnished bell tents, and a working organic farm. Exclusively yours — no other guests, no strangers, just the people who matter most.

Completely Private Site Hire

The bride-to-be deserves better than a shared campsite

Hopnest is a fully private riverside meadow on a 500-acre working organic farm in the Teme Valley. When you book, the entire site is exclusively yours — five furnished bell tents sleeping 10, a covered stretch tent for dining and dancing, a campfire, a rustic outdoor kitchen, hot shower and loos. No shared facilities, no neighbours, no strangers turning up.

It works beautifully for groups of 10 to 20. The five king-size bell tents sleep your core group in comfort, and there is space to bring extra air beds or small tents for the rest. The whole meadow, the campfire, the river — it all belongs to you for the duration of your stay.
