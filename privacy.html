Privacy Policy

At Hopnest Retreats, we are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, store, and process your personal data in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Data We Collect

We only collect the information necessary to manage bookings, contact lists, and payments. This includes:

  • Your Name, Email Address, Phone Number, and Billing Address.
  • Your selected booking dates, guest numbers, and any special requests you provide.
  • Your IP address and cookies used to improve site performance.

2. How We Collect Your Data

We collect data when you:

  • Complete a booking form on our website.
  • Join our waiting list popup to receive updates about stays.
  • Contact us directly via WhatsApp, email, or social media.

3. Payment & Third-Party Processors

  • Stripe: Your payment details are processed directly by Stripe. Hopnest Retreats never sees, processes, or stores your credit or debit card numbers on our servers.
  • Beds24: We use Beds24 as our booking system. Your booking information is securely shared with Beds24 to coordinate dates and sync with channels like Airbnb.
  • We will never sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties.

4. How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain your personal details for as long as necessary to process your booking, satisfy tax requirements, or keep you on our waitlist if requested. You can ask us to remove your contact details at any time by emailing hello@hopnestretreats.co.uk.

5. Your Rights

Under UK data laws, you have the right to access the personal data we hold about you, request corrections to incorrect data, or request complete deletion of your records. If you have any inquiries regarding your data, please contact Elizabeth.
