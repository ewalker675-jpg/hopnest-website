Our farm

Hopnest Retreats is located on a working organic farm in Worcestershire. We have farmed organically since 1998 and are one of only a handful of certified organic hop growers in the UK. Alongside our hop gardens, we keep pedigree Hereford cattle, EasyCare sheep, traditional orchards and species-rich wildflower meadows. Staying here gives you the chance to experience the countryside at a slower pace while supporting a family-run farming business.
