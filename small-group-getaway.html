The Small Group Getaway

A pair of bell tents on the river meadow, deep in the serene Teme Valley. Exclusively yours — for up to 8.

What's included

The Small Group Getaway is for the trips that matter — the post-uni reunion, the girls' weekend, the low-key stag, the dads' woodfire cooking, the mums' escape. Two furnished bell tents on a private riverside meadow in the Teme Valley, the whole spot yours alone, with no other guests to share it with.

This is the place to let your hair down and actually talk — the kind of long, winding, deep-and-meaningfuls you don't get round to in normal life. Feel the grass beneath your feet, light your own fire, cook over the flames, and stay up later than you meant to. No phones buzzing, no schedule, no strangers. Just your group, the meadow, and the river running past.

(For groups happy to share — the two tents sleep 8 across four beds, so it's made for friends who'd rather be together than tucked away in separate rooms.)

Inside the tents

  • 2 furnished bell tents, each with a king-size bed and a double bed (sleeps 8)
  • Bedding provided — bring your own towels
  • Per tent: king bed, double bed, bedside tables, under-bed storage, clothes rail, solar lighting

Outside (shared communal area)

  • Exclusive use of the riverside meadow — one booking at a time, no other guests
  • Private access to the River Teme — paddle, swim or just sit by the water (at your own risk)
  • Covered stretch tent for shelter and shade
  • Rustic covered outdoor kitchen — gas cooking, washing-up, somewhere to eat together
  • Fire pit — light your own fire and cook over the flames (firewood available on site)
  • One hot shower, one loo, free parking right by the meadow
