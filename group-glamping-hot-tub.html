Gather Around the Fire Pit

There is nothing quite like gathering around a warm fire pit after a day of exploring Martley and the River Teme. Our campfire area is the heart of the meadow stay, perfect for stargazing and spending quality time with friends.

Positioned right on the edge of our private riverside field, you can watch the sunset over the rolling Worcestershire hills and stargaze under completely unpolluted dark skies with your best friends.
