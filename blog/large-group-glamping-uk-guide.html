Large Group Glamping UK Guide: An Accessible Midlands Alternative to Scotland

Why choosing a central location in the Midlands can save your group hours of travel while offering the same wild countryside experience.

When planning a large group glamping holiday in the UK, many groups naturally search for locations in Scotland or deep in the countryside. While Scotland offers breathtaking wilderness, coordinating long journeys for a big group of friends or family members from different parts of the country can quickly become difficult.

Why Choose a Central Midlands Location?

Located in Martley, Worcestershire, Hopnest Retreats provides a highly accessible alternative right in the heart of England. Instead of spending 6 to 8 hours driving to Scotland, our location is just 25 minutes from Worcester, 50 minutes from Birmingham, and easily reachable from London and the South.

The Same Wild Countryside Experience

Choosing an accessible Midlands location doesn't mean compromising on the beauty of nature. Hopnest Retreats sits on a working, regeneratively managed organic farm since 1998, offering:

  • Total Privacy: Exclusive meadow hire with no other guests on site.
  • Riverside Setting: Direct access to the River Teme for paddling and relaxing by the water (at guests' own risk).
  • Comfortable Stay: 5 furnished bell tents sleeping 10 guests in king-size beds, with space for up to 20 total.
  • Exclusive Setting: A covered communal stretch tent, fire pits, hot showers, and flush toilets.
