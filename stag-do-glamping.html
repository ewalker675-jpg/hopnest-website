Stag Do Glamping in the Midlands — Exclusively Yours

A private riverside farm, five furnished bell tents, a campfire and outdoor kitchen. No other groups on site. No noise complaints. Central to Birmingham, Bristol, and the M5.

Full Site Exclusivity

Your group, your fire, your rules

When you book Hopnest, the entire site is yours. A private riverside meadow on a 500-acre working organic farm, five king-size bell tents, a covered stretch tent, a large fire pit, an outdoor kitchen with a gas hob, hot shower and flush loos. The River Teme runs along the edge of the meadow.

The farm is in the Teme Valley, Worcestershire — 25 minutes from Worcester, 50 minutes from Birmingham, an hour from Bristol. Central enough that groups driving from different cities can all get there without a four-hour slog. Clay pigeon shooting, fishing, and cycling are all bookable nearby. The Talbot country pub is a 5-minute walk.

Activities nearby

The daytime taken care of

Clay Pigeon Shooting

Several operators within 20 minutes of the farm offer half-day shoots for groups of any size. No experience needed — groups always love it.

River & Countryside

Fishing on the River Teme, cycling through the Teme Valley, and the Malvern Hills are all within easy reach. Walking routes start from the farm gate.

The Talbot Pub

Five minutes on foot from the meadow. A proper country pub with a good beer garden. Worth booking a table for your group dinner on the first night.
