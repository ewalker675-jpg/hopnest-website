# Hopnest Meadow Kitchen & Bell Tent Retreat: Pre-Opening Inventory Checklist

This inventory checklist is specifically tailored for the Meadow Glamping area, serving up to 20 guests across 5 bell tents. It covers the communal outdoor kitchen, fire pits, shared loos/showers, safety compliance, and site operations.

---

## 🍳 1. Meadow Communal Kitchen
*Durability, bulk capacity, and outdoor suitability are key here.*

### Cookware & Hobs
- [ ] **Gas Hob Cooking**:
  - [ ] 2x Large whistling kettles (gas compatible, 2.5L+ capacity)
  - [ ] 2x Extra-large stockpots with lids (for boiling pasta, potatoes, or stews)
  - [ ] 2x Medium saucepans with lids
  - [ ] 2x Large frying pans (heavy-duty, cast iron or professional stainless steel)
  - [ ] 1x Large cast-iron griddle plate/pan (versatile for hobs or fire pits)
- [ ] **Toaster**:
  - [ ] 1x 4-slice heavy-duty toaster (if mains power allows in the kitchen)

### Durable Tableware & Cutlery (Set for 24 – allows for breakage/spares, serves up to 20 guests)
- [ ] **Dinnerware (Melamine or Enamel)** – *Highly recommend heavy-duty enamel (e.g., Falcon enamelware) as it looks premium, is virtually unbreakable, and matches the rustic aesthetic:*
  - [ ] 24x Dinner plates
  - [ ] 24x Side plates
  - [ ] 24x Deep bowls (for chili, stews, cereal)
- [ ] **Outdoor-Safe Drinkware (Polycarbonate/Acrylic)** – *Do not use glass in the meadow area to avoid barefoot accidents:*
  - [ ] 24x Shatterproof wine/fizz glasses
  - [ ] 24x Shatterproof beer/water tumblers
- [ ] **Mugs**:
  - [ ] 24x Enamel mugs or extra durable ceramic mugs
- [ ] **Cutlery Set**:
  - [ ] 24x Dinner knives
  - [ ] 24x Dinner forks
  - [ ] 24x Dessert spoons
  - [ ] 24x Teaspoons
  - [ ] 2x Large cutlery sorting trays/baskets

### Food Prep & Utensils
- [ ] **Knives & Boards**:
  - [ ] Knife block set with sheathed chef's knives, bread knife, and utility knives
  - [ ] 3-4x Large wooden or heavy-duty plastic chopping boards (color-coded or labeled for meat/veg)
- [ ] **Prep Tools**:
  - [ ] 2x Box graters
  - [ ] 2x Y-shape vegetable peelers
  - [ ] 3x Multi-functional bottle openers & corkscrews
  - [ ] 2x Heavy-duty tin openers
  - [ ] 2x Garlic presses
  - [ ] 2x Kitchen shears/scissors
  - [ ] 2x Large colanders
  - [ ] 3x Large stainless steel mixing bowls
  - [ ] Salad servers & large salad bowl
- [ ] **Cooking Utensils**:
  - [ ] 3x Long-handled metal cooking tongs (essential for fire pits and hot grills!)
  - [ ] 2x Slotted spoons
  - [ ] 2x Large ladles
  - [ ] 2x Metal spatulas/turners
  - [ ] 2x Wooden spoons
  - [ ] 2x Trivets/heat mats

### Cleaning, Storage & Bins
- [ ] **Sink Station**:
  - [ ] 2x Large washing-up bowls
  - [ ] 2x Large wire dish-drying racks
  - [ ] Heavy-duty sponges, pot scrubbers, and dish brushes
  - [ ] Commercial/bulk size eco-friendly dish soap
  - [ ] 2x Hand wash dispensers
- [ ] **Linens**:
  - [ ] 8-10x Durable, quick-drying tea towels
  - [ ] 2x Large pairs of oven gloves
- [ ] **Food Storage & Protection**:
  - [ ] 4x Mesh fly/food covers (essential for keeping insects off food in an outdoor kitchen)
  - [ ] Set of plastic food storage containers (Tupperware)
  - [ ] Food bag clips
- [ ] **Waste Management**:
  - [ ] 1x Large heavy-duty general waste bin (with secure, animal-proof latch)
  - [ ] 1x Large heavy-duty recycling bin (with secure lid)
  - [ ] Extra-large bin liners

## 🔥 2. Communal Fire Pit Area
- [ ] 2x Pairs of **heavy-duty, heat-resistant leather fire gloves** (welder-style, for handling hot grills and logs safely)
- [ ] 1x Fire poker & ash shovel
- [ ] 1x Firewood storage basket / log holder
- [ ] 2x Refillable long-reach gas lighters (windproof)
- [ ] Marshmallow toasting forks (always a huge hit with groups!)

## 🚿 3. Shared Meadow Toilets & Showers
- [ ] **Shower Cubicle**:
  - [ ] 1x Large, quick-drying non-slip bath mat
  - [ ] Dispenser bottles for organic shampoo, conditioner, and body wash (refillable amber glass or plastic bottles with pumps)
  - [ ] Small bathroom bin (pedal style) & liners
  - [ ] Clothing/towel hooks (heavy-duty, weatherproof)
- [ ] **Toilet Cubicles (2 Loos)**:
  - [ ] 2x Toilet brushes & holders
  - [ ] 2x Toilet roll holders
  - [ ] 2x Hand soap dispensers
  - [ ] 2x Small sanitary bins & liners

## 🛟 4. Safety & Compliance
*Critical safety and compliance items required for public liability insurance.*

- [ ] **Meadow Communal Kitchen**:
  - [ ] 1x Carbon Monoxide (CO) detector (since kitchen is covered/semi-enclosed)
  - [ ] 1x Fire blanket (mounted near the gas hob)
  - [ ] 1x 2kg Dry Powder fire extinguisher
  - [ ] 1x Large family-sized British Standard compliant First Aid kit
  - [ ] 1x Emergency burns kit
- [ ] **Meadow Loos/Shower block**:
  - [ ] 1x 2kg Dry Powder fire extinguisher (mounted outside/near utility area)

---

## 📋 5. Operations & Guest Experience
- [ ] **Guest Information**:
  - [ ] 1x Weatherproof ring binder containing:
    - [ ] Local emergency numbers & directions to nearest A&E (Worcester/Malvern)
    - [ ] River safety rules (critical given river frontage)
    - [ ] Directions/guide to The Talbot pub (five-minute walk) and taxi contacts
    - [ ] Instructions for gas cookers and fire pits
- [ ] **Welcome Package Setup**:
  - [ ] Large wooden welcome crate/basket (for greeting the group with local cider/juice, eggs, bread)
- [ ] **Key Management**:
  - [ ] Key safes/lockboxes (if doing self-check-in) for the 5 bell tents
  - [ ] 5x Weatherproof key tags/fobs labeled with tent numbers/names
- [ ] **Signage**:
  - [ ] River safety signs ("No unsupervised swimming", "Deep water")
  - [ ] Farm/Meadow gates sign ("Please keep gate closed")
  - [ ] Waste/Recycling guidance signs
