Birthday Glamping · Worcestershire

Private Glamping for a Birthday to Remember

A 500-acre organic farm, a private riverside meadow, five furnished bell tents. Exclusively yours for the night — or the weekend. You've been planning this for months.

Exclusively Yours

The whole site, just for your group

From the moment you arrive, Hopnest belongs to you. Five king-size bell tents furnished with bedding, a covered stretch tent for your group dinner, a fire pit for late-night stories, outdoor kitchen with gas hob, hot shower and flush loos. The river is a five-minute walk through the meadow.

Groups of 10 come and sleep in the five bell tents. groups of up to 18 are welcome — bring extra sleeping arrangements and the stretch tent easily holds everyone for a birthday meal. Local caterers deliver, grazing boards arrive, and The Talbot pub is a 5-minute walk for a pre-dinner drink.
