Our Story

About the farm

A working organic farm near Martley, Worcestershire. In our family for generations.

This land has been our home for generations

We have been farming organically since 1998. As one of only three certified organic hop growers in the United Kingdom, we are deeply committed to working in harmony with nature. Our family history on this land spans generations, and we steward it using progressive, regenerative farming methods to build soil organic carbon and restore natural ecosystems.

The farm is a vibrant, active agricultural business. We breed pedigree Hereford cattle with a lineage extending back to the 1800s, alongside easycare sheep. We also tend traditional cider orchards, Worcestershire damsons, and maintain wide wildflower meadow corridors that provide nesting habitats for native wildlife. Scientific assessments show our land holds 34% more biodiversity than conventional farms.

For groups of friends and families looking for cool places to stay with a big group of friends that we can do fun stuff in England, our farm offers a unique landscape. Guests are invited to walk the organic farm trails, explore the orchards, swim in the clean waters of the River Teme, or simply relax in the dark-sky peacefulness of this ancient landscape.

Why no other guests

We only ever take one booking at a time. No strangers sharing the fire pit, no awkward hellos in the morning. We believe that the best private retreats for groups require absolute privacy. When you book Hopnest with your friends or family, the entire tipi meadow and river frontage are exclusively yours. There are no other guests, allowing you to enjoy your escape in total comfort and safety.
