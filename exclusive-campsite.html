About the Campsite

Wide open space. Entirely yours.

This is a genuine off-grid camping experience set on 1.5 acres of lush, privately managed land in the heart of Worcestershire — sitting right alongside the River Teme. When you book, the entire site is yours and yours alone. There are no other guests, no shared spaces, and no neighbours to worry about. Just your group, the countryside, and a proper campfire under an open sky.

This is camping as it should be. We provide the essentials and take care of the land; the rest is up to you to bring, just like any great campsite experience. Perfect for large friend groups, extended families, birthday weekends, festivals of your own making, or anyone who wants true outdoor freedom without splitting into different accommodation.

⚠ Important — River Use The campsite is situated next to the River Teme. Any access to or use of the river is entirely at guests' own risk. Please exercise caution and supervise children at all times near the water.
