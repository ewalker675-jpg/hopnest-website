What's included

The Bell Tent Retreat offers exclusive glamping in Worcestershire for families, friends and groups looking for a private countryside escape. Your booking includes exclusive use of approximately three acres of private riverside meadow, with no shared facilities and no other guests on site. Surrounded by grazing livestock, hay meadows and the wider landscape of our working organic farm, it offers the feeling of having your own corner of the Worcestershire countryside.

Inside each bell tent

  • King-size bed
  • Bedding provided
  • Bedside tables
  • Under-bed storage
  • Clothes rail
  • Solar lighting

Outside (shared communal area)

  • Covered stretch tent
  • Fire pit (firewood available to buy on site)
  • Rustic covered outdoor kitchen with gas cooking, washing-up facilities and dining space
  • Two loos
  • Hot shower
  • Free parking — drive straight onto the site
  • Private access to the River Teme for relaxing by the water, paddling and enjoying the riverside setting (all river activities at guests' own risk)
