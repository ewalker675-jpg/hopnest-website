Hen Parties & Group Hires

A Beautiful, Safe & Private space to Celebrate

Are you planning a hen party or a special weekend with 10 to 20 friends in Worcestershire? Hopnest offers a completely private riverside bell tent meadow where your group can laugh, cook, and relax in total privacy. There are no other guests or strangers on site, ensuring you have the entire space to yourselves.

Gather under our spacious covered stretch tent, relax in the wood-fired hot tub overlooking the Teme Valley, share stories around the campfire, or try wild swimming on the River Teme.
